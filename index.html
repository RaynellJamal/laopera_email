The History of Mozart and Da Ponte

There are so many elements that go into making a classic opera. While a brilliant composer is a must there should also be a gripping story to highlight the genius music. Lorenzo Da Ponte is most famous for being the librettist for three of Mozart's greatest operas (The Marriage of Figaro, Don Giovanni, and Cosi Fan Tutte).

Join us as we take a brief look at this pair's fascinating history as well as Da Ponte's influence on Mozart's masterpieces.

Meet the Don Giovanni Cast: Alan Williams

We've had the recent pleasure to sit down and talk with the young and talented Alan Williams who is playing Masetto in our Don Giovanni production. As an LA native himself, Williams shared some of his favorite LA activities as well as his fascinating pre-show ritual and his dream revenge song for Giovanni as the Don receives his comeuppance. Williams will be making various appearances on the main stage this season so let's get to know the artist portraying these incredible characters.
